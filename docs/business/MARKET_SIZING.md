# Market sizing + ops model — templated one‑page previews

## Target segments (initial ICP)
1) **Small local services**: electricians, plumbers, salons, dentists, gyms, small hotels/apartments
2) **Micro‑B2B**: small agencies, trades, consultancies
3) **Event organizers**: festivals, conferences, local events (short‑lived landing pages)
4) **Personal branding**: coaches, therapists, artists

## Why they buy
- They have **no website** or an outdated one; need credibility + contact channel.
- They don’t want a big project; they want **fast + cheap + done**.

## Funnel assumptions (starting point)
- Leads sourced/month: **500** (scrape + directories + manual qualification)
- Contact rate (deliverable preview link successfully sent): **70%**
- Preview view rate: **25%**
- Reply rate: **6%**
- Close rate from replies: **25%**
- Net conversion from leads → paid: **0.25%–1.5%** (start low, improve with targeting)

## Pricing (illustrative)
- **Setup fee:** 150€ (one‑time)
- **Subscription:** 25€/month (hosting + maintenance)
- Optional tiers:
  - 49€/mo: analytics + 1 small edit/month
  - 99€/mo: content refresh + monthly report

## Unit economics (illustrative)
Assume 150€ setup + 25€/mo, retention 24 months.
- Year‑1 revenue/customer: **150€ + 12×25€ = 450€**
- Gross CLTV (24m): **150€ + 24×25€ = 750€**
- Costs per customer (rough):
  - hosting+infra: **~0.20–1.50€/mo** (static hosting scales very cheaply)
  - support: target **<15 min/mo** average
- Net CLTV depends on support intensity + CAC.

## Hosting + support cost notes
- Static hosting (Cloudflare Pages / Netlify / Vercel) keeps costs very low.
- Biggest risk is **support/time**, not infra. Protect with:
  - tight scope
  - templated content
  - paid edit packages

## Key KPIs
Growth:
- leads/month, previews sent, preview view rate
- reply rate, close rate, CAC
Revenue:
- setup revenue, MRR, ARPA, churn, net revenue retention
Ops:
- time‑to‑preview, time‑to‑launch, tickets/site/month, minutes/site/month
Quality:
- refund rate, NPS

## Notes
- TAM is geography‑dependent; start with one city/region and iterate.
